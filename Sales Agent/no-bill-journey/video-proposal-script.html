No-phone / low-call leads

Video proposal — assignment after the design

Replace static PDFs with a ~5 minute screen-share walkthrough of their design. Close with a verification call as a requirement for contract release — and an assignment to reply “Ready” after watching.

Suggested structure

  • Open — “Hi [Name], I’ve reviewed your bill and uploads.”
  • Bill logic — “Here’s how we sized this from your usage and tariffs.”
  • Design — “This is why your No-Bill system looks like this on the plan.”
  • Mindset — “No-Bill is a 7-year financial guarantee — it needs us in the driver’s seat on optimisation; that’s non-negotiable for the $0 outcome.”
  • Respect preference — “I know you prefer not to jump on a call yet — that’s fine for this stage.”
  • Gate — “Because this is a 7-year guarantee, I can’t release the final contract until a 10-minute verification call so we can confirm your roof photos match our satellite data and assumptions.”
  • Assignment — “Reply with the word Ready after you’ve watched this, and we’ll send a link to book that 10-minute slot.”

Full script draft

Hi [Name], I’ve gone through your electricity bill and the photos you sent — thanks for getting those over. On screen is the design we’d stand behind for your No-Bill guarantee. I’m going to walk through, in plain language, why the system size and battery control strategy look like this for your usage and your network tariffs — not a generic “solar + battery” layout. A quick heads-up on how Reposit works: we’re not selling a one-off install and walking away. We’re taking responsibility for your bill outcome. That means we need control of how the battery is run day to day, and it means we’re picky about data — which is why we asked for your bill and site photos up front. I know some people prefer to review everything without a call first. Totally fine — that’s what this video is for. The part I can’t move on: before we release a final contract, we need a short, roughly ten-minute verification call. This is a seven-year financial guarantee; I have to confirm your roof and switchboard match what we’ve assumed from satellite imagery and your photos. No sales ambush — just verification. When you’ve watched this, please reply with the word Ready and we’ll send you a link to grab a 10-minute slot. Thanks, [Your name] Reposit Power
