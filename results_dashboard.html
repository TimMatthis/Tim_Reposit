Assumptions vs. Reality

Digital Twin Assumptions

  • Perfect Foresight: The battery knows the exact solar and load profile for the day in advance.
  • Synthetic Profiles: Solar is a perfect sine wave; Load is a standard double-peak profile.
  • Perfect Hardware: 100% uptime, fixed efficiency (90%), no degradation.
  • Simplified Tariff: Flat Time-of-Use rates (Peak/Off-peak) and fixed Feed-in Tariff.

Real World Errors & Variances

  • Prediction Errors: Weather forecasts and user behavior are unpredictable.
  • Profile Volatility: Clouds cause solar intermittency; appliances cause load spikes.
  • Hardware Reality: Inverter faults, communication loss, capacity degradation.
  • Tariff Complexity: Demand charges, block tariffs, and dynamic spot pricing.
