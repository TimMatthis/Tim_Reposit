Fleet headline margin & implied monthly fee

Uses the same economics as the fleet run: net margin is feed-in credits minus grid charges (gross_revenue − gross_cost). Headline margin is that net figure as a percentage of either activity (charges + credits) or gross feed-in credits only, matching margin_pct_activity / margin_pct_revenue on each home.

This page shows two views: the simulated numbers from the optimiser, and reality-adjusted numbers that apply the delivery-rate haircut learned from comparing simulated vs actual billed performance across the fleet. The adjusted view answers: "given what we know about model accuracy, what fee is needed to hit the margin target in the real world?"
