Fleet import & export prices (aggregate)

For each calendar month and each day, prices are volume-weighted across every home in the export set: fleet import $/kWh = (sum of import_price × import energy) ÷ (sum of import energy), and similarly for exports. All optimisation_intervals.csv rows in that period are pooled (this can take a minute on large fleets).
