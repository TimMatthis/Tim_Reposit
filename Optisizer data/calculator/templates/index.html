Fleet averages (mean per home)

Fleet averages (mean per home)

Activity margin = net margin ÷ (gross charges + gross credits) × 100. It measures the share of all money flowing through the meter that the home retains as profit (or loses). This is the default margin reported throughout the page; "% rev." (÷ revenue only) appears in some tables for comparison. No-trade columns appear when interval CSVs include a retail-without-trade-back counterfactual (see API schema); batch comparisons can add the same story under retail_without_trade in the batch JSON.
