What is the Reposit Controller?

It's a small hardware device - about the size of a Wi-Fi router - that our team installs directly onto your existing battery cabinet. It connects your battery to the wholesale energy market, allowing Reposit to trade on your behalf 24/7. Your existing battery, inverter and solar setup stay exactly as they are.
